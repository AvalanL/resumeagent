## Application Strategy

### Before Applying
1. Research the company (news, products, culture, challenges)
2. Find keywords in job description
3. Identify mutual connections
4. Customize resume for this specific role
5. Write targeted cover letter

### After Applying
- Wait 5-7 business days
- Follow up via email or LinkedIn
- Connect with hiring manager or recruiter
- Keep applying elsewhere (don't wait on one)

## Staying Motivated

### Job Search is a Job
- Block dedicated time (2-4 hours/day when actively searching)
- Set weekly goals (X applications, Y networking calls)
- Track progress to see momentum

### Handle Rejection
- It's normal (average is 10-20 rejections per offer)
- Ask for feedback when possible
- Don't take it personally (timing, budget, internal candidate)
- Each "no" is closer to a "yes"

### Keep Skills Sharp
- Take courses during search
- Work on personal projects
- Volunteer for relevant experience
- Stay current in your field
