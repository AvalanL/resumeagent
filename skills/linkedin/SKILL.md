# LinkedIn Optimization Skill

You transform LinkedIn profiles into job magnets.

## Trigger

User says `/linkedin` or wants LinkedIn help.

## The 7 Key Sections

### 1. Profile Photo

**Guidelines:**
- Professional headshot (face fills 60% of frame)
- Good lighting, plain or blurred background
- Friendly expression, approachable
- Recent (within 2-3 years)
- No sunglasses, no group shots, no logos

**Tip:** Profiles with photos get 21x more views.

### 2. Banner Image (Background)

**Ideas:**
- Your industry/field visual
- City skyline (your location)
- Abstract professional design
- Company logo if employed
- Personal brand statement

**Size:** 1584 x 396 pixels

### 3. Headline (Most Important!)

**Formula options:**

```
[Role] | [Specialty] | [Value Proposition]
```
Example: "Senior Product Manager | B2B SaaS | Turning User Insights into Revenue"

```
[Action] + [Who You Help] + [Result]
```
Example: "Helping Startups Launch Products That Users Actually Want"

```
[Title] at [Company] | [Passion/Side Focus]
```
Example: "Marketing Director at Acme Corp | Speaker | AI Enthusiast"

**Max 220 characters. Make them count.**

### 4. About Section (Summary)

**Structure (2,600 character max):**

**Paragraph 1 — Hook**
Your story, why you do what you do.

**Paragraph 2 — What You Do**
Core skills, expertise, specialties.

**Paragraph 3 — Accomplishments**
2-3 key achievements with metrics.

**Paragraph 4 — What You're Looking For** (if job seeking)
Type of role, industries of interest.

**End with CTA:**
"Let's connect: [email]" or "DM me about [topic]"

**Write in first person.** "I" not "John is a..."

### 5. Experience Section

**For each role:**
- Job title (use searchable terms)
- Company (link to company page)
- Duration
- 3-5 bullet points with accomplishments
- Media attachments (presentations, articles, projects)

**Mirror resume content but more conversational.**

### 6. Skills Section

- Add 50 skills (max allowed)
- Top 3 are pinned — choose strategically
- Get endorsements (ask colleagues)
- Include keywords recruiters search

**Find keywords:** Look at job postings for your target role.

### 7. Featured Section

**Showcase:**
- Articles you've written
- Media mentions
- Portfolio pieces
- Presentations (SlideShare)
- Certifications
- Notable projects

**This is your highlight reel.**

## Profile Optimization Checklist

- [ ] Custom URL (linkedin.com/in/yourname)
- [ ] Location set correctly
- [ ] Industry selected
- [ ] "Open to Work" enabled (if applicable)
- [ ] Contact info complete
- [ ] 500+ connections (for credibility)
- [ ] Recommendations (at least 3)
- [ ] All sections completed

## For Job Seekers

**Enable "Open to Work":**
- Choose visible to recruiters only (private)
- Or green badge (public) — signals urgency

**Keywords matter:**
Recruiters search by:
- Job titles
- Skills
- Company names
- Certifications
- Location

**Sprinkle these throughout your profile.**

## Activity That Gets Noticed

1. **Post 2-3x per week** (thoughts, industry news, wins)
2. **Comment on others' posts** (thoughtful, not "Great post!")
3. **Share articles** with your take
4. **Celebrate others** (congrats on new roles)
5. **Join relevant groups**

## Output

Provide:
1. **Optimized headline** (2-3 options)
2. **Complete About section**
3. **Skills list** (top 20 to add)
4. **Recommendations request template**
5. **Profile review checklist**
